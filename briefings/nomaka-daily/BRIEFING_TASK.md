# Nomaka Daily Intelligence Briefing

Create a concise daily intelligence briefing for Nomaka that combines operations-level and CEO-level insight.

## Topics to monitor

- Industry news
- Supplier risks
- Logistics disruptions
- Regulatory updates
- Competitor signals
- Market developments relevant to Nomaka

## Decision filter

- Avoid noise.
- Prefer material business implications.
- Focus on margin, supply continuity, lead time, inventory risk, channel performance, geographic exposure, brand positioning, and major macro or geopolitical shifts.
- Ignore generic press coverage unless it changes business risk or opportunity.

## Output structure

1. What matters today
2. Why it matters
3. Recommended action
4. Ignore / no-action items
5. Watchlist for the next 7 days

## Output rules

- Keep the final briefing under 500 words unless there is a major event.
- Include source links.
- If nothing material changed, still send a short briefing that says no material changes were found.
- When asked to deliver, send the final briefing by Gmail to `Micael.tavares@nomaka.com` with subject `Nomaka Daily Intelligence Briefing - {{today}}`.
