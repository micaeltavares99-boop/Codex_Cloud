# Repository Guide

This repository contains Nomaka operational tooling plus a repository-backed daily intelligence briefing pack.

## Briefing Files

Use [briefings/nomaka-daily/BRIEFING_TASK.md](briefings/nomaka-daily/BRIEFING_TASK.md) when the task is to produce the Nomaka daily intelligence briefing.

## Briefing Expectations

- Combine operations-level and CEO-level signal.
- Prioritize business implications over generic headlines.
- Cover industry news, supplier risks, logistics disruptions, regulatory updates, competitor signals, and market developments relevant to Nomaka.
- Keep the final briefing under 500 words unless there is a major event.
- If there are no material developments, still produce a short briefing stating that no material changes were found.
- Include source links.
- When the task explicitly asks for delivery, send the briefing by Gmail to `Micael.tavares@nomaka.com`.

## Edit Boundaries

- Keep briefing-related changes inside `briefings/`.
- Do not modify unrelated operational scripts unless the task explicitly asks for it.
